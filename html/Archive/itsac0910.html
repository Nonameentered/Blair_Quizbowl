Formats

  • Tossups - 50 questions, 10 points each
  • Knowledge Master Open - 200 questions, 5 points for a first try answer with up to 5 points for a fast answer, 2 points for a second try answer
  • Quizmaster Challenge (QC) - 2 four-question category individual rounds per team, 20 points each; 12-question opening round, 10 points each; 10-question lightning round, 15 points each; 10-question bonus round, 15 points per tossup, up to 30 points per bonus
  • It's Academic - each team starts with 100 points; questions are abandoned with incorrect answers as well as correct ones (no bounceback); 8-question competitive category round, 10 points each, -10 points for incorrect answer; 5-question individual round, 20 points each; 8-question competitive visual round, 10 points each, -10 for incorrect answer; 8-question individual round, 20 points each, 25 point bonus for all 8 correct; competitive grab bag round until time expires, 20 points for correct answer, -20 points for incorrect answer, 30 points for visual questions, -30 for incorrect visual answer
  • National Academic Quiz Tournaments/Academic Competition Federation (NAQT/ACF) - 24 or 20 questions, respectively, 10 points each, +5 points for early answer ("power"), -5 points for incorrect answer if question is interrupted, 30-point bonus available for each correct answer
