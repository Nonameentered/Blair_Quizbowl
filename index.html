Welcome to the somewhat new and vastly improved (but still stylistic outdated) MBHS It's Academic/Quizbowl website!

Quizbowl is an academic competition covering subjects from history, science, and literature to pop culture. Click here to learn about the game.

We play in many tournaments throughout the school year, and a team of three people plays on the It's Academic television show! Click here to check it out.

We welcome everyone, regardless of skill level. All you need is a willingness to learn and an eagerness to have fun. Blair's It's Academic team is sponsored by Blair's Diversity Coordinator Rahman Culver.
