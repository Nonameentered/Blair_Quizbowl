2017-2018

The 2018 High School National Championship Tournament was held in Atlanta, Georgia on 5/26 and 5/27. Blair's A Team placed 12th in a field of 352 teams. The B Team placed 77th and the C Team placed 175th.

The 2018 PACE National Scholastic Tournament was held in Reston, Virginia on 6/2 and 6/3. Blair's A Team placed 10th in a field of 96 teams. The B Team placed 49th.

The team won the It's Academic finals and superbowl once again.
